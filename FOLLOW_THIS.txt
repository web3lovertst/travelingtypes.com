STEP 1: Open cPanel
-------------------
- Log into your cPanel account
- You should see many icons/buttons

STEP 2: Find "Addon Domains" or "Subdomains"
----------------------------------------------
- Look for a button/icon that says "Addon Domains" 
- OR look for "Subdomains"
- Click on it

STEP 3: Find Your Domain
-------------------------
- You should see a list of domains
- Find "travelingtypes.com" in the list
- Look for a button next to it that says "Change" or "Modify" or "Edit"
- Click that button

STEP 4: Change the Path
------------------------
- You will see a box that says "Document Root" or "Root Domain"
- DELETE what's in that box
- TYPE THIS EXACTLY:
  repositories/travelingtypes.com/backend/public

STEP 5: Save
------------
- Click the "Change" or "Save" button
- Wait 5 minutes
- Visit travelingtypes.com in your browser
- It should work!
