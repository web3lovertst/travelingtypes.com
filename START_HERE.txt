STEP-BY-STEP (Copy this and follow it):

1. Open cPanel (log in)

2. Click on "Addon Domains" (or "Subdomains" if you see that instead)

3. Find "travelingtypes.com" in the list

4. Click "Change" or "Modify" next to it

5. Find the box that says "Document Root"

6. Delete what's in that box

7. Type this EXACTLY:
   repositories/travelingtypes.com/backend/public

8. Click "Save" or "Change"

9. Wait 5 minutes

10. Visit travelingtypes.com - it should work!
